Services & Features

We are on a mission to maximize resource value from electronic and other dry waste – scrap, while minimizing, and even eliminating negative environmental impact so that both our economy and our environment can thrive.

Waste Collection

We will pick up your scrap from your doorstep. Just initiate a request, either on our website or through our app available on playstore.

Environmental Impact

We help protect our environment by ensuring a reduced carbon footprint and hence a sustainable future.

Recycling Services

We recycle all your waste – electronic, plastic, glass, et al in an efficient and effective manner in keeping with our environmental commitments.

Refurbished Products

In keeping with our aim and resolve of protecting our environment, we produce new goods from the recycled scrap.

Authorised Partners

All our scrap is recycled by our CPCB-authorised Recycling Partners with our plants at IMT Manesar, Haryana and Kushkhera, Rajasthan.

Sustainability

Along with recycling waste, we make sure to create a positive variation in our environment by planting a sapling for every 5 kilograms of scrap we process.

How We Work

 

Scrap Pickup Request

Initiation of a waste pickup request on our website or app

Onsite Scrap Collection

Provision of onsite waste pickup services upon request

Payment to Client

Payment to the client according to the estimated scrap value

Waste Recycling

Waste recycling through our CPCB certified recycling partners

Refurbishing Products

Sale of products produced from the waste recycled by us

Reduced Carbon Footprint

Our recycling efforts ensure a reduced carbon footprint
